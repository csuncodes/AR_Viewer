About.

Here are an example of the collection of 3D molecules created at Brandeis University as a tool for conducting teaching and research.

To view a model, simply click on the picture of the molecule you would like to view. You will then be transported to that molecule's AR page. Here, you can view the molecule in augmented reality using the provided QR code to observe the molecules from different angles and distances. At the university, students are able to checkout physical, 3D printed versions of these models for a better understanding of their structure and behavior.
